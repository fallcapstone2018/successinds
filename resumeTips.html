Resume Tips

With hundreds of other applicants competing for the same job as you, you need to be a step above the rest. To make a great first impression on potential employers, you need to have a resume that stands out.

Getting Started

Typically, every resume has a set of guidelines or rules that need to be followed. These rules provide a set of standards that every employer looks for in a resume. Below is a list of these standards that every Digital Sciences Student should follow. There is also a checklist provided below to make sure you have the best resume possible.



Resume Rules of Thumb

  • Keep the resume to ideally one page but possibly two if front and back

  • Must not have any spelling or grammatical errors

  • Have consistant spacing and have the headings highlighted

  • The content on your resume must be accurate and honest

  • Print your resume on quality paper

  • Cater your resume to the industry you are applying towards

  • If sending in resume online, always send it as a PDF

  • Key words are important to use in your resume as many employers scan for specific words

How To Stand Out On A Resume

Standing out on your resume is the best way to get your potential employers attention. There are many different things you can do to be ahead of the rest. Below are a few things that Kent State University provides to better yourself for your future.



Join A Organization

joining an organization is a great way to show off on your resume that you are involved with your concentration outside of classes. Below are a list of organizations that can relate back to your Digital Sciences degree that can also be a talking point during your interview.
  • Interested in game design? Animation And Game Design Club is a great club to learn new tools in the industry! Click Here for more information.
  • Get to know other Digital Scientists in your Major when you join Digital Scientists. A great way to network within your degree. Click Here for more information.
  • Love to code? HacKSU is a great organization to learn and improve upon your coding skills. Click Here for more information.
  • Want to network with great companies within the area? M.I.S.A. is a great organization to learn and potentially land a internship from various companies in northeast Ohio. Click Here for more information.
  • Interested in UX Design? UX Design KSU is a great organization for learning UX design and networking with professional within the field. Click Here for more information.


Take Part In An Internship

Having an internship is crucial for a college student. It shows you have experiential learning and sets you apart from others who potentially do not. Going to Kents Career Fair is a great way to find an employer that will help you grow. If your internship goes well you might even be offered a full time position when you graduate. Click Here for more information regarding internships.


Get An Experts Opinion

Getting an outside opinion on how your resume looks is a great way to improve it. This person can tell you all the areas that could use work along with telling you how. Having the perfect resume is crucial for getting that first job out of college so dont let this resource go to waste. Kent State University offers this resource at their Career Exploration And Development Center. Click Here to set up an appointment today.
