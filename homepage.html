Resume Tips

Creating a resume that stands out is essential to the businesses that review them.

When creating a resume, you need to stand out from the rest to catch the eyes of employers. Having organizations, past internships, and having a bit of creativity are all posible ways to stand out

  • If you have a profolio, include a link to it
  • Keep the resume ideally to one page
  • Always include clubs or organizations
  • Try to only include skills related to the job
  • Print on quality paper
