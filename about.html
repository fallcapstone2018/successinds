About Our Project

Our group decided to create a website that would help Digital Science Students become sucessfull not only in thier Major/Concentration but also successful in finding a career after graduation.

Why we chose to create this website

Kent States Digital Science page on thier website is somewhat confusing to navigate and find useful information. To find the information you need it takes a great deal of digging through the website to find what you were looking for. So, we decided to create a website that highlights important information that up an coming students and current students would find very useful.

Before we decided to create our website we went to Kent States Digital Science page. We went through each of the pages and figured out what things were missing and what things could be added to make the website more useful.

Here are a few thing we found that Kent States Digital Science website that can be added/fixed:

  • The "Why Digital Sciences?" page gives an ACCESS DENIED when navigating to the page

  • It would be useful to have an Internships page that explains the importance of getting an internship to improve finding a career after graduation

  • Example of what careers you can go into depending on the concentration you choose

  • Tips on creating a strong resume

  • Guide on how to obtain an internship. The steps of finding, preparing and succeeding in finding a internship

  • The overall website is bulky and confusing to navigate to find the information you are looking for
