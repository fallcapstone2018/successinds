Internships

Make yourself stand out with an internship to show your skills

Why Do An Internship?

There are many reasons for Digital Sciences students to strongly consider applying for an internship.

  • Extremely valuable hands-on experience in the field.
  • Recieve 5 upper-division credits toward Graduation.
  • Get your foot in the door at a major company.
More

What To Look For

There are a wide variety of internships available in the tech world.

  • Make sure you work in a field related to what you're studying.
  • An organization with room for growth, that hires interns.
  • An organization with plenty of networking opportunities.
More

Useful Tools

There are many tools and services you can use to find the right internship for you.

  • Interact with companies and recruiters using LinkedIn.
  • Attend the Career Fair to meet major companies and apply.
  • Ohio Means Internships can be used to find local opportunities.
More

Interview for Internship

The Interview will be your first major impression on a company.

  • Research the company and learn about their organization.
  • Bring your resume and a notepad with questions you have.
  • Follow up with the company with a "Thank You" letter 24 hours after.
